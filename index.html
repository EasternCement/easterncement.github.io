Welcome to Eastern Cement Company

The Eastern Province Cement Company was established by the Royal Decree No. M/11 dated 14/03/1402 H.
The Paid up Capital is SR 860 Million consisting of 86,000,000 shares owned by Citizens and official. 
The company is founded mainly to produce different kinds of Clinker and Cement to cater the need of local and Gulf market.

Production Records

The Company commenced commercial production in 1985 and according to records, the production reached 1,449,442 tons clinker and 862,605 tons cement, that year. By the Grace of God, production increased in following years and effective 1994, the Clinker production reached 2,300,000 tons, crossing the installed capacity of 2,200,000 M Tons. The plant achieved a record clinker production of 2,357,423 tons in 1995, which is 12.26% more than the nominal capacity. Similarly in 2001, one Kiln achieved a run factor of 344 days, which is a new benchmark in any standard in cement industry. Similarly the highest cement production of 2,916,364 tons was recorded in the year 1994. The high quality of the products is also maintained, which received international fame in the world market. The Company has been exerting continued efforts for increasing production as well as maintaining the high quality of its product standard. Entire plant is operating on its optimum level of production.
